Our Dentist

Dr. Annahvathany Sivakumaran

Dr. Anna earned her Doctor of Dental Medicine degree at the University of British Columbia, Canada (2012). Prior to this, she completed her Honours Bachelor degree for Dental Surgery at University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka (2002).

Following her degrees, Dr. Anna gained experience as an assistant lecturer in Oral Medicine and Radiology, and as an associate dentist in several dental clinics, before opening Courtyards Dental Center.

Dr. Anna prioritizes listening to her patients and carries sound diagnostic judgement. She is a certified member of the Royal College of Dental Surgeons of Ontario and the Ontario Dental Association. She also completed the FORCE Orthodontic Program.
